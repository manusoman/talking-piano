What is this?

Talking Piano is inspired from this video by Mark Rober. Watch it - it's great!

The app listens to what you say and then mimics it just using piano notes! To play, first record your voice by pressing and holding the "Record" button. Make sure that you speak in a clear and loud enough voice. The piano will start playing your voice back to you once you release the button. If you want to hear it again, press the "Play" icon next to the "Record" button. If you feel anything wrong, just press the "loudspeaker" icon which will play your voice back to you, so that you can check if the voice was recorded well.

Also, note that the app needs microphone access to record your voice.
