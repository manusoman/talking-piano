What is this?

Talking Piano is inspired by this video from Mark Rober. Watch it, it's really great!

Basically, Talking Piano listens to what you say and mimic it by only using its keys! To play, fist record your voice by pressing and holding the "Record" button. The piano will start playing your voice back to you once you let go of the record button. If you wanna hear it again, press the "Play" button next to the "Record" button. If you feel anything wrong, just press the "Check" button which will play your voice back to you, so that you can see if the voice was recorded well.

Warning: this app isn't perfect and you would find issues. Please keep your expectations low ;)
